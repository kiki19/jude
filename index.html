Unsure of his life's direction, ayoung Danny Thomas sought guidance from St. Jude Thaddeus, the patron saint of hopeless causes.

If the saint would point to the path he should take, Danny vowed to build a shrine in his name.

Danny Thomas officially opened St. Jude Children's Hospital on February 4,1962.

In 2017,US News and World Report ranks St Jude Childrens Research Hospital the #1 Childrens Hospital for Cancer in the country.
